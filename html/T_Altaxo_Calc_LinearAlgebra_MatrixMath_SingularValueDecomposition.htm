Some properties of the singular value decomposition:
             X - the matrix to decompose, U w V' - the decomposition.

                   -1
             (X' X)    = V (1/w^2) V'  (usually called covariance matrix)

                                    -1
             Hat matrix H = X (X' X)  X' = U U'
