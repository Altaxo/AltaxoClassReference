ToComplex(String)
Creates a complex number based on a string. The string can be in the following formats (without the quotes): 'n', 'ni', 'n +/- ni', 'ni +/- n', 'n,n', 'n,ni,' '(n,n)', or '(n,ni)', where n is a double.
