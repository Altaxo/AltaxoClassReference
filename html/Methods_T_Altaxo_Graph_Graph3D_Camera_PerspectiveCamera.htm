ZoomByGettingCloserToTarget Zooms the view by moving the camera along the straigth line between camera and target position closer to the target, and then slightly rotate the camera so that the relative screen points rx and ry are matched before and after the zooming (for a point at the plane at camera distance). The target position itself is kept.
ZoomByMovingCameraForward Zooms the view by moving the camera forward, i.e. by shifting both eye position and target position by the same amount, thus keeping the camera direction and the up vector constant.
