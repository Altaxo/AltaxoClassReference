Gets the intended parameter boundaries. This are soft limits, boundaries so that the intended purpose of the fit function is fullfilled. Example: in the exponential decay Exp(-a*t) a is intended to be positive. This is a soft limit, and not a hard limit, because a could be also negative, and the fit nevertheless would succeed.
