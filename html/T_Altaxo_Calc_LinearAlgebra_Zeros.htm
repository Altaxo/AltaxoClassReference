Zeros Enumeration

Zeros Enumeration

Designates the mode for enumeration and mapping of matrix elements.

Namespace:  Altaxo.Calc.LinearAlgebra
Assembly:  AltaxoCore (in AltaxoCore.dll)
Syntax
C#
Copy
public enum Zeros
Members
  Member nameValueDescription
AllowSkip0 Allow skipping zero entries, without enforcing it. When enumerating or mapping sparse or banded matrices, this can speed up operations.
Include1 Force applying the operation to all fields even if they are zero.
AllowSkipButIncludeDiagonal2 Force applying the operations to all fields in the diagonal even if they are zero, additionally to all non-zero fields.
