Parameters

g  Graphics
The graphic contents to paint to.
bForPrinting  Boolean
Indicates if the painting is for printing purposes. Not used for the moment.
Remarks
The reference point (0,0) of the GraphDocument is the top left corner of the printable area (and not of the page area!). The hosting class has to translate the graphics origin to the top left corner of the printable area before calling this routine.
