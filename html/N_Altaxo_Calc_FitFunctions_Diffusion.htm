Classes
 ClassDescription
MassChangeAfterExponentialEquilibrationForCylinder Describes the mass change of a cylinder (with given radius) in a diffusion process after a concentration change that is modeled by an exponential equilibration.
MassChangeAfterExponentialEquilibrationForPlaneSheet Describes the mass change of a plane sheet (with given thickness and infinite lateral dimensions) in a diffusion process after a concentration change that is modeled by an exponential equilibration. The diffusion process occurs on both planes of the plane sheet.
MassChangeAfterExponentialEquilibrationForSphere Describes the mass change of a sphere (with given radius) in a diffusion process after a concentration change that is modeled by an exponential equilibration.
MassChangeAfterStepForCylinder Describes the mass change of a infinite long cylinder (with given radius) in a diffusion process after a concentration step change.
MassChangeAfterStepForPlaneSheet Describes the mass change of a plane sheet (with given thickness and infinite lateral dimensions) in a diffusion process. The diffusion takes place on both sides of the sheet. The diffusion process occurs on both planes of the plane sheet.
MassChangeAfterStepForSphere Describes the mass change of a sphere (with given radius) in a diffusion process after a concentration step change.
