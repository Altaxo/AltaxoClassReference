Gets a value indicating whether the ridge line has at least a signal the provided signal-to-noise ratio. The signal-to-noise ratio is calculated at the point of the lowest stage (lowest width, see PointAtLowestWidth).

Gets a value indicating whether the ridge line has at least a signal-to-noise ratio greater than or equal to the provided value. The signal-to-noise ratio is calculated at the point with the first maximum of the Cwt coefficient (see PointAtMaximalCwtCoefficient).
