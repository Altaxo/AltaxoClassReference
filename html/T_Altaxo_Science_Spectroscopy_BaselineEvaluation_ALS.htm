ALS Class

Implements the Asymmetric Least Squares method for baseline estimation, proposed by Eilers and Boelens (2005) [1].

P Gets or sets the weighting parameter. The default value is 0.1. A value of 0.5 leads to symmetric weighting of positive and negative deviations. Values less than 0.5 lead to stronger suppression of (positive) peaks.
