Laplace Class

Continuous Univariate Laplace distribution. The Laplace distribution is a distribution over the real numbers parameterized by a mean and scale parameter. The PDF is: p(x) = \frac{1}{2 * scale} \exp{- |x - mean| / scale}. Wikipedia - Laplace distribution.
